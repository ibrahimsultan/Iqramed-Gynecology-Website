 7) What are the eligibility criteria for the course?

Practicing radiologists, gynecologists, sonologists, or medical students, desiring to learn or enhance their skills in Obstetric & Gynecological ultrasound or in managing infertility, from anywhere in the world, can enroll for the course.

8) What certificate will I get on completing the courses?

There are a variety of certificates offered by the academy based on the course you opt for.
There is no certificate issued for individual lectures.
Certificate of Participation will be provided for all courses if the self-assessments of all the lectures in the course are correctly answered.
Certificate of Completion and Competence is only available to those opting for the three major courses - Fetal Medicine & Obstetric Ultrasound, Gynecological Ultrasound or Infertility Management. If, in addition to the self-assessments of all the lectures in the course, image submission is completed with all images accepted by the faculty, in order to procure a Certificate of Completion. For Certification of Competence in addition to self-assessment and image submission you require to pass the final examination scoring 50% or above.
